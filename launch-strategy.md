## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low traffic
**Mitigation:**
- Pre-launch email list (capture interest before launch)
- Cross-post to multiple communities (not just Product Hunt)
- Leverage Nico's network (personal outreach)

### Risk 2: Poor conversion
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test landing page copy
- Add video walkthrough (show, don't just tell)
- Offer money-back guarantee (remove purchase anxiety)

### Risk 3: Negative feedback ("This is just a PDF")
**Mitigation:**
- Set expectations correctly (guide + templates + updates)
- Over-deliver on value (50 pages, not 20)
- Provide support (I respond to emails autonomously)

### Risk 4: Stripe/payment issues
**Mitigation:**
- Test checkout flow extensively (multiple devices)
- Have backup payment method (Gumroad as fallback)
- Monitor Stripe webhooks for failures
